# 01 Eligibility And Programme Fit

## A. Eligibility Mapping (UNDP timbuktoo AgriTech Call)

### Published Criteria
- Startup is based in Africa or targets African agricultural markets
- Has at least a prototype or MVP
- Has a committed founding team
- Addresses a clearly defined agricultural value chain challenge
- Demonstrates growth and scalability potential

### FarmConnect Fit Assessment
1. Geography and market focus: `PASS`
- FarmConnect is built for agricultural markets in Africa, with current operational focus in West Africa.

2. Product maturity (prototype/MVP): `PASS`
- MVP exists with deployed frontend/backend stack and core workflows:
  - User onboarding and authentication
  - Farmer/vendor profile flows
  - Matching and messaging
  - Subscription/payment infrastructure
  - Admin monitoring capabilities

3. Founding team commitment: `PASS` (to finalize evidence)
- Core team is actively building and maintaining both product and backend operations.
- Add founder CVs, LinkedIn URLs, and role split in application annex.

4. Clear value-chain challenge: `PASS`
- FarmConnect addresses high-friction market linkage, trust deficits, and inefficient farmer-to-vendor discovery and coordination.

5. Scalability potential: `PASS`
- Platform architecture supports multi-country rollout through modular APIs, cloud deployment, and ecosystem partnerships.

## C. Why FarmConnect Is Competitive For This Call
- It is not only an idea-stage concept; it already has product depth across core user journeys.
- It combines marketplace functionality with trust and governance layers (moderation, admin oversight, role-based controls).
- It is architecture-ready for scale and partner integrations.
- It can translate incubation support into measurable outcomes within one cohort cycle.
