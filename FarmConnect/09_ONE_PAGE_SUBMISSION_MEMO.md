# 09 One-Page Submission Memo

## FarmConnect
FarmConnect is a live AgriTech platform that helps farmers, vendors, and service providers discover one another, build trust, and coordinate transactions more efficiently across African agricultural markets.

## Executive Positioning
FarmConnect is not a directory or static marketplace. It is a workflow-driven market access and trust layer for agriculture — designed to reduce search friction, improve counterpart confidence, and create repeatable commercial relationships that can scale across regions.

## Why This Fits timbuktoo
The timbuktoo AgriTech programme backs startups that can improve productivity, strengthen food systems, raise farmer incomes, and support climate-resilient agriculture. FarmConnect aligns directly with this mandate by addressing a core value-chain bottleneck: reliable market access and coordination.

## Current Product State
- Live public demo: https://farmapp.kwezitechnologiesltd.africa
- Public API health endpoint is available and responsive
- Core journeys are implemented:
  - Signup and login
  - Farmer/vendor onboarding
  - Matching and messaging
  - Admin oversight and moderation
  - Payment/subscription infrastructure

## Readiness Snapshot
FarmConnect is in a strong pre-beta stage and is scheduled for a controlled beta in approximately two weeks.

Evidence of readiness:
- Demo is publicly reachable
- Critical product flows are operational
- Backend health endpoint is live
- Role-based controls and moderation are in place
- Infrastructure is cost-disciplined and suitable for controlled learning

## Problem Solved
FarmConnect reduces the time, trust friction, and coordination burden involved in identifying relevant agricultural counterparties. That improves deal flow, strengthens business relationships, and supports more reliable participation in agricultural markets.

## Why It Stands Out
- Built as a full workflow, not a static listing site
- Modular full-stack architecture supports scale and iteration
- Governance and moderation layers improve trust and safety
- Clear path to measurable user, business, and impact outcomes

## Infrastructure Context
- Hosted on Render free tier for controlled validation
- Uses MongoDB and RabbitMQ in a lean operating setup
- Ready for beta testing with a managed cohort and explicit upgrade triggers

## Two-Week Beta Focus
Over the next two weeks, FarmConnect will focus on:
- Stabilizing the critical user journey
- Verifying reliability under beta load
- Capturing early evidence of adoption and product-market fit
- Preparing the team for incubation support and investor follow-up

## Support Requested From The Programme
We are seeking incubation support to:
- Strengthen product and user experience
- Improve market activation and distribution
- Build rigorous impact reporting
- Prepare for investment readiness and scale

## Submission-Ready Closing
FarmConnect is ready for structured incubation support. It has a live MVP, a clear agricultural market need, a differentiated product direction, and a practical beta path. With timbuktoo support, FarmConnect can move from functional MVP to a scalable AgriTech platform with measurable economic and social impact.
