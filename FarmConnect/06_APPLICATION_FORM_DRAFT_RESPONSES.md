## 2. One-Line Description
FarmConnect is an African AgriTech platform that helps farmers and vendors discover trusted partners, coordinate transactions, and improve market access outcomes.

## 3. Problem You Are Solving
Smallholder farmers and agri-vendors face fragmented market access, low trust in counterparties, and high coordination friction. These barriers reduce deal flow, increase transaction risk, and limit income opportunities. FarmConnect addresses these constraints with a structured digital workflow that improves discovery, matching, and trusted engagement.

## 4. Your Solution
FarmConnect provides:
- Profile-based farmer/vendor onboarding
- Intelligent matching workflows
- In-platform communication with trust and governance layers
- Admin oversight and analytics to support quality and scale

Our approach is practical and execution-focused: we digitize the steps where agricultural market linkages most often fail.
