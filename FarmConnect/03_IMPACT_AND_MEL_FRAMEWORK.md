# 03 Impact And MEL Framework

## A. Theory Of Change

### Problem
Smallholder farmers and agri-vendors often face fragmented market access, low trust, and inefficient transaction coordination.

### Intervention
FarmConnect provides a digital platform for profile-based matching, communication, and trust reinforcement across agricultural actors.

### Immediate Outputs
- Farmers and vendors onboarded with complete profiles
- Match opportunities created and activated
- Communication and transaction intent workflows initiated

### Short-Term Outcomes
- Reduced time-to-discovery for relevant market counterparts
- Increased interaction quality and confidence in counterpart reliability
- Increased share of users progressing from registration to active engagement

### Medium-Term Outcomes
- Higher transaction conversion rates
- Improved vendor utilization and farmer access to services/inputs/offtake channels
- Income opportunity improvements for participating users

### Long-Term Impact
More inclusive, resilient, and efficient agricultural market systems that support productivity and livelihood growth.

## C. Inclusion Indicators
Track disaggregated metrics by:
- Gender
- Age band (including youth)
- Geography (rural/peri-urban/urban)

Recommended inclusion KPIs:
- Share of women-led farmer/vendor profiles
- Share of youth-led profiles
- Share of active users from underserved areas

## D. Climate-Relevant Indicators (Roadmap-Compatible)
- Number of users receiving climate-relevant recommendations (if enabled)
- Share of users adopting climate-smart practices informed by partners/content
- Reported reduction in climate-related disruption for connected transactions

## E. MEL Operating Rhythm
1. Monthly
- KPI extraction, variance review, corrective actions

2. Quarterly
- Outcome review with partner feedback and user interviews

3. End of incubation
- Cohort impact snapshot + investor-facing traction narrative

## F. Data Quality Principles
- Define each KPI with one owner and one source of truth
- Separate verified metrics from estimates
- Preserve an auditable metric changelog
- Use confidence labels: `Verified`, `Sample-based`, `Assumed`

## G. Evidence Artifacts For Incubation Reporting
- KPI dashboard snapshots
- Product funnel exports
- User testimonials and case studies
- Partner validation letters or MOUs
- Before/after operational stories from pilot users
