# 04 Implementation Plan And Budget Framing

## A. 6-Month Incubation Workplan

### Month 1: Baseline And Focus
- Finalize priority user segments and pilot geographies
- Establish baseline metrics and MEL instrumentation
- Run UX and onboarding optimization sprint
- Confirm partner pipeline for distribution

### Month 2: Pilot Activation
- Launch targeted onboarding campaigns
- Activate first structured pilot cohorts
- Train users on core workflows
- Begin partner-supported field feedback loops

### Month 3: Conversion Optimization
- Improve matching quality and funnel conversion
- Refine trust/safety workflows and moderation tuning
- Deploy retention nudges and re-engagement flows

### Month 4: Expansion Readiness
- Prepare second geography or segment expansion
- Standardize operational playbooks for partner onboarding
- Publish first validated impact snapshot

### Month 5: Investment Readiness
- Build data room and investor narrative
- Develop financial model and growth assumptions
- Stress-test unit economics and CAC pathways

### Month 6: Demo And Scale Plan
- Consolidate traction evidence
- Deliver final incubation report and pitch deck
- Define post-incubation scale roadmap and capital plan

## B. Milestones
- M1: Baseline dashboard live
- M2: First cohort onboarded
- M3: Conversion KPI uplift validated
- M4: Expansion playbook completed
- M5: Investor materials finalized
- M6: Demo-day ready package completed

## C. Budget Framing (Use Of Support)

### Recommended Allocation Buckets
1. Product and engineering hardening: 30%
- Feature reliability, analytics instrumentation, integrations

2. Market activation and partnerships: 25%
- User acquisition pilots, community activation, field engagement

3. Monitoring, evaluation, and learning: 10%
- Data ops, surveys, reporting, evidence generation

4. Trust, compliance, and operations: 15%
- Moderation quality, governance, policy and process development

5. Team capability and venture readiness: 20%
- Founder coaching, business model refinement, investor readiness

## D. Resource Ask Narrative (For Application)
FarmConnect is seeking incubation support to move from MVP to repeatable, evidence-backed growth. The support will be used to strengthen product reliability, accelerate market validation through partner-led pilots, and establish robust impact and business metrics that position the venture for regional scaling and follow-on investment.

## E. Key Assumptions To Validate During Incubation
- Segment-specific onboarding conversion rates
- Match-to-transaction intent conversion rates
- Retention dynamics across farmer and vendor cohorts
- Partnership channels with best acquisition efficiency

## F. Go/No-Go Triggers
- If month-3 conversion KPI uplift is below threshold, narrow to one highest-performing segment.
- If user trust score drops, prioritize trust workflow improvements before expansion.
- If CAC trend exceeds model assumptions, pivot toward channel partnerships.
